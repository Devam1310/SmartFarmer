Tomato

Diseases like Fusarium wilt, Late blight and Septoria leaf spot are found in tomato plant.

Grapes

Diseases like Aspergillus rot, Black rot of grapes and shoestring root rot are found in grape tree.

Orange

Diseases like Anthracnose Colletotrichum gloeosporioides, Melanose Diaporthe citri and Brown rot Phytophthora spp are found in orange tree.

Soybean

Diseases like frogeye leaf spot, brown spot and downy mildew are found in Soybean tree.

Squash

Diseases like Anthracnose, Downy mildew and Powdery mildew are found in squash tree.

Potato

Diseases like Rhizoctonia canker, Rosellinia black rot and Powdery scab are found in potato tree.

Corn

Diseases like Gray leaf spot, Common rust and Southern corn leaf blight are found in corn tree.

Maize

Diseases like Southern rust, Anthracnose and Gray leaf spot are found in maize tree.

Strawberry

Diseases like Leaf spots, Powdery mildew and Grey mold are found in strawberry tree.

Peach

Diseases like Bacterial Canker, Brown Rot and Peach Scab are found in peach tree.

Apple

Diseases like Apple Scab, Collar rot and Sooty block are found in apple tree.

Blueberry

Diseases like blueberry rust are commonly found in blueberry tree.

Cherry

Diseases like Cytospora Canker, Silvery Leaf and Brown Rot are found in cherry tree.

Pepper

Diseases like Charcoal rot, Root rot and Anthracnose are found in pepper tree.

Raspberry

Diseases like Spur blight, Silvery Leaf and Brown Rot are found in raspberry tree.
